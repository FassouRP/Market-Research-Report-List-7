<p><p>The industrial grade carbon fiber market is primarily divided into two types: regular-tow carbon fiber and large-tow carbon fiber. Regular-tow carbon fiber contains fiber strands with smaller diameters, offering high strength and stiffness, making it suitable for applications requiring precision and performance, such as aerospace and automotive industries. In contrast, large-tow carbon fiber features wider strands, which provide a cost-effective option for bulk applications, like sporting goods and industrial components, without significantly compromising performance or durability.</p></p>
