<p><p>Crimped end mailing tubes are categorized into three types based on their construction: Single Ply, Double Ply, and Triple Ply. Single Ply tubes consist of a single layer of material, making them lightweight and economical for lightweight shipments. Double Ply tubes offer added strength with two layers, suitable for moderate protection of contents. Triple Ply tubes provide the highest durability with three layers, ideal for heavy or fragile items requiring maximum protection during transit. Each type caters to specific shipping needs.</p></p>
