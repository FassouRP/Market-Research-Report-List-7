<p><p>The laser diffraction diameter market includes three main types of probes: single probe, dual probe, and wide-angle photoelectric probe. Single probes measure particle size using a single laser beam, suitable for consistent sample types. Dual probes enhance accuracy by using two beams to measure size variability in diverse samples. Wide-angle photoelectric probes capture data from multiple angles, providing a comprehensive particle size distribution analysis. Each type serves specific applications across industries like pharmaceuticals, materials science, and food processing.</p></p>
