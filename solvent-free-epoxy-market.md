<p><p>The solvent-free epoxy market includes several types, each with unique characteristics. Water-based epoxy uses water as a solvent, making it environmentally friendly. Conventional solvent types contain traditional solvents for application ease. High solid solvent types have a higher resin content, resulting in thicker coatings. Powder coating types involve epoxy powders that are thermally cured, providing durable finishes. Lastly, radiation curing types utilize UV or electron beams for quick curing, ensuring efficient production and high-performance coatings.</p></p>
