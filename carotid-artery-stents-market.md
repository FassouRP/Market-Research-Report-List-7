<p><strong>The Carotid Artery Stents Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Balloon-Expandable Stents</li><li>Self-Expanding Stents</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The carotid artery stents market consists of two primary types: balloon-expandable stents and self-expanding stents. Balloon-expandable stents are placed in a collapsed state and then expanded using a balloon catheter, providing precise deployment and diameter flexibility. In contrast, self-expanding stents are made from materials that allow them to expand automatically after placement, conforming to the vessel's shape. Both types aim to maintain proper blood flow and reduce the risk of stroke in patients with carotid artery disease.</p></p>
