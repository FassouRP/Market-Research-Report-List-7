<p><p>The Gamma Glutamyl Transferase (GGT) testing market is primarily divided into two types: ELISA-based and colorimetric-based tests. ELISA-based tests utilize enzyme-linked immunosorbent assay technology for high sensitivity and specificity in measuring GGT levels, making them suitable for clinical diagnostics. Colorimetric-based tests, on the other hand, employ color change reactions to quantify GGT concentration, offering simplicity and rapid results. Both methods are essential in liver function assessment and diagnosing related diseases, catering to diverse healthcare needs.</p></p>
