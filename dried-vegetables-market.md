<p><ul><li>Freeze Dried Vegetables</li><li>Air Dried Vegetables</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dried vegetables market comprises primarily two types: freeze-dried and air-dried vegetables. Freeze-dried vegetables undergo a process that involves freezing them and then removing moisture under a vacuum, preserving their taste, color, and nutrients. They are lightweight and have a long shelf life, making them ideal for camping and emergency supplies. Air-dried vegetables, on the other hand, are dehydrated using heated air, resulting in a more concentrated flavor but potentially lower nutrient retention. Both types cater to convenience and demand for healthy, shelf-stable food options.</p></p>
