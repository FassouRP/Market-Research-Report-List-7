<p><ul><li>Absorbable</li><li>Non-absorbable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D printing medical devices market is divided into two main types: absorbable and non-absorbable. Absorbable devices, often made from biocompatible materials, gradually dissolve in the body over time, eliminating the need for surgical removal and minimizing long-term complications. In contrast, non-absorbable devices are designed to remain permanently in the body, offering durable solutions for various medical applications. Both types enhance patient outcomes by enabling personalized treatments and precise designs tailored to individual anatomical needs.</p></p>
