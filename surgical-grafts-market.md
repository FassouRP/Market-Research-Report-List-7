PTFE (polytetrafluoroethylene) grafts offer superior chemical resistance and minimal tissue adherence, making them ideal for vascular applications. Other grafts may include biological grafts, absorbable materials, or combinations of synthetic and natural substances, catering to diverse surgical needs and patient requirements in different medical procedures.</p></p>
