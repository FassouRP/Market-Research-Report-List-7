<p><p>The Optical Epoxy Molding Compound (EMC) market is primarily divided into two types: Solid Optical EMC and Liquid Optical EMC. Solid Optical EMC is a thermosetting polymer that is pre-mixed and set into a solid form, providing excellent mechanical and optical properties for various applications. Conversely, Liquid Optical EMC is a resin-based material that can be poured into molds, allowing for complex shapes and designs. Both types cater to industries requiring high-performance optical components, enhancing light transmission and durability.</p></p>
