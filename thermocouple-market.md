<p><p>A thermocouple is a temperature measuring device that consists of two different metal wires joined at one end, creating a junction. When this junction experiences a temperature change, it generates a voltage that can be correlated to temperature differences. Thermocouples are widely used in various industries, including manufacturing, aerospace, automotive, and energy, due to their versatility, durability, and ability to measure a broad temperature range.</p><p>The thermocouple market is poised for significant growth, expected to expand at a CAGR of 12.4% during the forecast period.
