<p><p>Bivalirudin is a synthetic anticoagulant medication used primarily during percutaneous coronary interventions (PCI) and in patients with heparin-induced thrombocytopenia (HIT). It functions as a direct thrombin inhibitor, preventing blood clot formation by binding to thrombin, a key enzyme in the coagulation cascade. Its short half-life and reversible action make it a preferred choice in certain clinical settings, particularly for patients at risk of bleeding complications.</p><p>The Bivalirudin Market is experiencing steady growth, driven by the increasing prevalence of cardiovascular diseases, rising demand for minimally invasive procedures, and advancements in anticoagulant therapies.
