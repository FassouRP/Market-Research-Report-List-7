<p><p>Emission control systems encompass various technologies designed to reduce pollutants released into the atmosphere. Electrostatic precipitators use electric charges to remove particles from exhaust gases. Absorbers capture gaseous emissions by passing them through a liquid solution, neutralizing harmful substances. Catalytic reactors facilitate chemical reactions to transform pollutants into less harmful byproducts. Catalytic converters, primarily used in vehicles, convert harmful gases from combustion engines into less toxic emissions, enhancing air quality and compliance with environmental regulations.</p></p>
