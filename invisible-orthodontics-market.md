<p><p>The invisible orthodontics market encompasses three primary types: ceramic braces, clear aligners, and lingual braces. Ceramic braces utilize transparent materials, offering a discreet alternative to traditional metal braces. Clear aligners, like Invisalign, are removable and virtually invisible, providing a comfortable, flexible treatment option. Lingual braces are placed behind the teeth, making them hidden from view. Each type caters to patients seeking effective teeth straightening solutions that maintain aesthetics during the orthodontic treatment process.</p></p>
