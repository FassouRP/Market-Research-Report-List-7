<p><ul><li>Oil-based Fluids (OBF)</li><li>Water-based Fluids (WBF)</li><li>Synthetic-based Fluids (SBF)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The drilling fluids market comprises three primary types: Oil-based Fluids (OBF), Water-based Fluids (WBF), and Synthetic-based Fluids (SBF). OBFs offer good lubrication and shale stability, making them suitable for challenging formations. WBFs are more environmentally friendly, cost-effective, and typically used in shallow wells. SBFs combine the advantages of both OBFs and WBFs, providing superior performance in various conditions while minimizing environmental impact. Each type caters to different drilling needs based on geological and operational requirements.</p></p>
