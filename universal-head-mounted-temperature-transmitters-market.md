<p><strong>The Universal Head Mounted Temperature Transmitters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>Universal Head Mounted Temperature Transmitters are categorized into three main types based on their wiring configurations: 2-Wire, 3-Wire, and 4-Wire. </p><p>2-Wire transmitters use two wires for both power supply and signal transmission, making them cost-effective and simple to install, but they may have limited accuracy. </p><p>3-Wire transmitters offer improved accuracy by utilizing an additional wire for the signal, reducing the effects of resistance. </p><p>4-Wire transmitters provide the highest precision with separate wires for power and signal, ideal for critical applications requiring minimal error.</p></p>
