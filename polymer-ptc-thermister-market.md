<p><p>The Polymer PTC Thermistor market is categorized into two main types: Surface Mount Type and Radial Leaded Type. Surface Mount Thermistors are designed for compact, space-constrained applications, offering ease of integration into multilayer circuit boards. In contrast, Radial Leaded Thermistors feature leads extending vertically, making them suitable for through-hole mounting, providing robust connections in various electronic devices. Both types utilize polymer materials for their positive temperature coefficient properties, enabling efficient thermal management in applications ranging from consumer electronics to automotive systems.</p></p>
