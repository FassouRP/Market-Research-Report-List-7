<p><p>The linear rotary actuator market comprises three main types: pneumatic, hydraulic, and electric actuators. Pneumatic actuators use compressed air to generate motion, offering rapid response and clean operation, suitable for lighter loads. Hydraulic actuators utilize pressurized fluid, providing high force and precision, ideal for heavy-duty applications. Electric actuators convert electrical energy into motion, offering precise control, efficiency, and ease of integration with automation systems, making them versatile for a wide range of industrial applications. Each type serves distinct functionality and efficiency requirements.</p></p>
