<p><p>The digital orthodontics market primarily consists of two key types: digital orthodontics scanners and digital orthodontics imaging devices. Digital orthodontics scanners are advanced tools designed to capture precise 3D digital impressions of a patient's teeth and oral structures, enhancing treatment planning and facilitating clear aligner fabrication. On the other hand, digital orthodontics imaging devices include tools like intraoral cameras and radiographic imaging systems that provide detailed visualizations of dental conditions, aiding in diagnosis and treatment progression monitoring. Both types improve workflow efficiency and patient outcomes.</p></p>
