<p><p>The undercast padding bandage market features two primary types: flexible fabric bandages and cohesive fixation bandages. Flexible fabric bandages are designed to provide comfort and adaptability, allowing for movement while offering cushioning and support over casts. Cohesive fixation bandages adhere to themselves, ensuring a secure fit without sticking to the skin or underlying dressings. Both types play a critical role in the management of casts, enhancing patient comfort and promoting effective healing by providing protection and support to injured areas.</p></p>
