<p><p>The automotive brake hoses and lines market is primarily divided into two types: rubber and braided stainless steel. Rubber hoses are flexible, lightweight, and cost-effective, making them commonly used in standard vehicles. They provide good performance but may degrade over time due to heat and environmental factors. Braided stainless steel lines, on the other hand, offer increased durability, strength, and resistance to abrasion and pressure changes, leading to improved braking performance. They are often preferred in high-performance and modified vehicles.</p></p>
