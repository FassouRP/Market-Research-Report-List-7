<p><p>Drop-in fuels for aviation encompass various sustainable aviation fuel (SAF) types that can seamlessly replace conventional jet fuel without requiring significant modifications to aircraft engines or infrastructure. </p><p>- HEFA-SPK (Hydroprocessed Esters and Fatty Acids Synthetic Paraffinic Kerosene) originates from renewable lipids.</p><p>- FT-SPK (Fischer-Tropsch Synthetic Paraffinic Kerosene) is derived from biomass via gasification.</p><p>- ATJ-SPK (Alcohol-to-Jet Synthetic Paraffinic Kerosene) is produced from ethanol or other alcohols.</p><p>- Co-processing blends traditional jet fuel with bio-based components.</p><p>- Other includes emerging or unique SAF technologies under development.</p></p>
