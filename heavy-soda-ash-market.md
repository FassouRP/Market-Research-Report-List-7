<p><p>The Heavy Soda Ash market is primarily produced through two methods: the Solvay Method and the Trona Method. The Solvay Method involves a chemical process using limestone, salt, and ammonia to produce soda ash, widely used in glass manufacturing and chemicals. The Trona Method extracts natural trona ore, which is processed into soda ash, offering cost and energy efficiency. Both methods cater to industries like detergents, glass, and chemicals, with the Trona Method gaining preference due to its environmental and economic advantages.</p></p>
