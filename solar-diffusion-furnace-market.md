<p><p>The solar diffusion furnace market is categorized into vertical and horizontal types based on their design and operation. Vertical solar diffusion furnaces are oriented upright, allowing for efficient thermal distribution and compact designs, often used in high-throughput applications. In contrast, horizontal solar diffusion furnaces are laid out flat, facilitating easier handling of substrates and uniform heating. Both types serve the semiconductor and photovoltaic industries, optimizing processes for doping and material diffusion, contributing to improved solar cell performance.</p></p>
