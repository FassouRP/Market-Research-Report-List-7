<p><p>The seismic base isolation system market comprises three main types: Natural Rubber Bearings (NRB), Lead Rubber Bearings (LRB), and High Damping Rubber (HDR) bearings. NRBs provide flexibility and energy dissipation through natural rubber, effectively reducing seismic forces on structures. LRBs incorporate lead plugs for enhanced damping, offering superior performance during strong earthquakes. HDRs utilize high-damping materials to absorb vibrations, ensuring stability. Together, these systems are crucial for protecting buildings and infrastructure from seismic activity while maintaining structural integrity and safety.</p></p>
