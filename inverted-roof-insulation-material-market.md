<p><p>The inverted roof insulation material market comprises various types, primarily focused on Extruded Polystyrene (XPS) and Expanded Polystyrene (EPS). XPS is known for its consistent thickness, moisture resistance, and high compressive strength, making it ideal for flat roofs. EPS, while generally less expensive, offers good thermal performance and lower weight but has a higher moisture absorption rate. Additionally, the "Others" category includes alternative materials such as polyisocyanurate and rigid foam boards, catering to diverse insulation needs and preferences in roofing applications.</p></p>
