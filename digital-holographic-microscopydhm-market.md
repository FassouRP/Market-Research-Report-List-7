The market for DHM is primarily categorized into two types: label-free interferometric techniques, which enable real-time observation of living cells without invasive methods, and quantitative phase imaging, which provides precise measurements of cell structures and dynamics, enhancing applications in biological research, medical diagnostics, and material science.</p></p>
