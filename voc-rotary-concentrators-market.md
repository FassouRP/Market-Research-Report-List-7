<p><p>The VOC rotary concentrators market is primarily categorized into two types: Zeolite VOC rotors and Activated Carbon VOC rotors. Zeolite VOC rotors utilize zeolitic materials to adsorb volatile organic compounds, offering high performance in moisture-laden environments and enabling effective thermal desorption. Activated carbon VOC rotors, on the other hand, rely on activated carbon's porous structure to capture VOCs, providing robust adsorption capabilities and thermal regeneration. Both types serve to enhance air quality and reduce emissions in industrial applications.</p></p>
