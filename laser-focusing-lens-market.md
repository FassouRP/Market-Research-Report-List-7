<p><ul><li>Plano-convex Lens</li><li>Meniscus Lens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laser focusing lens market primarily includes plano-convex lenses and meniscus lenses, each designed for specific applications. Plano-convex lenses feature one flat surface and one outwardly curved surface, effectively converging light, making them ideal for beam collimation and focusing applications. Meniscus lenses, on the other hand, are curved on both sides—one concave and one convex—allowing for more compact designs and reduced distortions in certain optical systems. Both types are essential for enhancing laser performance across various industries.</p></p>
