<p><p>The System-on-Chip (SoC) market is categorized into three types: MSI (Medium-Scale Integration), LSI (Large-Scale Integration), and VLSI (Very-Large-Scale Integration). MSI integrates hundreds of transistors, suitable for basic applications. LSI incorporates thousands, enabling more complex functionalities and designs. VLSI, with millions of transistors, facilitates advanced processing capabilities, supporting modern applications like smartphones and AI devices. Each type serves specific needs based on complexity, required performance, and application area, driving innovation in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
