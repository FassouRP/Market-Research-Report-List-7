<p><p>Electrical Discharge Machining (EDM) is a non-traditional manufacturing process that utilizes electrical discharges or sparks to remove material from a workpiece. This method is particularly effective for machining hard metals and complex geometries, making it widely used in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and electronics. The system consists of a power supply, a tool electrode, and a workpiece submerged in a dielectric fluid, which enables precise control of the machining process.</p><p>The Electrical Discharge Machining System Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.1% during the forecast period. <p><p>The Electrical Discharge Machining (EDM) system market comprises various types, primarily Wire-cut EDM and Forming EDM. Wire-cut EDM utilizes a thin, electrically charged wire to precisely cut complex shapes from conductive materials, ideal for intricate designs. In contrast, Forming EDM employs specially shaped electrodes to produce desired contours and features on workpieces. Both methods leverage controlled electrical discharges to remove material without physical contact, enhancing precision and surface finish in metalworking applications across industries.</p></p>
