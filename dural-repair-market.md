<p><p>The Dural Repair Market is categorized into two primary types: Biologic Dural Repair and Synthetic Dural Repair. Biologic Dural Repair utilizes natural materials, such as human and animal tissues, to promote healing and tissue regeneration. These materials are often preferred for their compatibility with human biology. In contrast, Synthetic Dural Repair involves the use of artificial materials, often polymers, designed to mimic the properties of natural dura. These offer durability and flexibility, catering to diverse clinical needs in neurosurgery.</p></p>
