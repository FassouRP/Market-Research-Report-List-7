<p><p>Horse racing markets offer various betting options. A Win Bet is a straightforward wager on a horse to finish first. An Each Way Bet covers both winning and placing, typically in the top spots. A Single Bet involves one selection, while Multiple Bets combine several selections for potentially higher payouts. A Straight Forecast predicts the exact order of the top two finishers, while a Reverse Forecast allows for those two horses to finish in either order. Tricast requires predicting the top three finishers in exact order.</p></p>
