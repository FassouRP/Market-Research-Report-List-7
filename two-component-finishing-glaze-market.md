Oil-based finishing glazes offer durability and a rich finish, making them suitable for high-traffic areas and outdoor applications. They typically have a longer drying time but provide excellent resistance to wear and moisture. In contrast, water-based finishing glazes are environmentally friendly, with lower VOC emissions and quicker drying times.
