<p><p>High Electron Mobility Transistors (HEMT) are a type of transistor technology known for their high-speed performance and efficiency, ideal for applications in radio frequency (RF) and microwave communications. HEMTs are widely utilized in sectors such as telecommunications, aerospace, defense, and automotive industries, particularly for power amplification and signal processing.</p><p>The High Electron Mobility Transistors (HEMT) Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.5% during the forecast period.
