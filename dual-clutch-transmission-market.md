<p><ul><li>Wet Dual Clutch Technology</li><li>Dry Dual Clutch Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dual clutch transmission (DCT) market is segmented into two main types: wet dual clutch technology and dry dual clutch technology. Wet dual clutch systems utilize coolant oil, which helps with heat dissipation and allows for higher torque handling, making them suitable for high-performance vehicles. In contrast, dry dual clutch systems lack coolant, resulting in lighter weight and simpler design, typically found in smaller, more fuel-efficient cars. Both types enhance shift speed and driving performance, catering to diverse automotive needs.</p></p>
