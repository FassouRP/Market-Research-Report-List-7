<p><ul><li>Powdered Concentrate</li><li>Frozen Concentrate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The citrus juices market features two primary types: powdered concentrate and frozen concentrate. Powdered concentrate involves dehydrating citrus juice to create a soluble powder, which can be easily transported and reconstituted with water for consumption. Frozen concentrate, on the other hand, is produced by removing water from fresh citrus juice, then freezing it for preservation. Both forms offer convenience and a longer shelf life compared to fresh juice while retaining essential flavors and nutrients, making them popular in food and beverage applications.</p></p>
