Flow cytometry measures cellular characteristics, aiding in cell analysis. Next-generation sequencing (NGS) enables rapid DNA sequencing for genomics studies, while polymerase chain reaction (PCR) amplifies DNA for various applications. Microscopy offers high-resolution imaging of biological samples, enabling detailed visualization and analysis of cellular structures. Together, these technologies advance life science research and diagnostics.</p></p>
