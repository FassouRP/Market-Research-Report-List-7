<p><p>The EUV pellicle market is segmented based on transmittance levels, reflecting the efficiency of light passage through the pellicle. Pellicles with ≥90% transmittance are ideal for high-resolution lithography, minimizing signal loss. Those in the 85%~90% range offer a balance of protection and performance for advanced applications. Pellicles with 80%~85% transmittance may be used in less critical layers, while ≤80% transmittance pellicles are typically less favorable, as they can significantly affect image quality and precision in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
