<p><p>The swimming pool control panels market is primarily divided into two types: fixed and mobile control panels. Fixed control panels are permanently installed at the pool site, offering stable management for water quality, temperature, lighting, and filtration systems. These panels typically feature advanced automation and user interfaces for enhanced user experience. In contrast, mobile control panels provide flexibility, allowing users to manage pool systems remotely via smartphones or tablets, making them ideal for homeowners who prioritize convenience and control from various locations.</p></p>
