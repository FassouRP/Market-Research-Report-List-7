<p><p>The Plastic Alloy Market involves blending different polymers to enhance material properties. Physical methods include mechanical mixing, extrusion, and injection molding, where polymers are combined without altering their chemical structure, focusing on improving physical characteristics like strength and flexibility. Chemical methods involve reactive blending, grafting, or copolymerization, where chemical reactions modify polymer chains to create new materials with tailored properties, such as enhanced thermal stability or chemical resistance. Both approaches aim to produce advanced plastic alloys for diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
