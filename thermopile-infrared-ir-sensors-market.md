<p><p>The Thermopile Infrared (IR) Sensors market is primarily categorized into two types: Through Hole Thermopile IR Sensors and SMD (Surface-Mount Device) Thermopile IR Sensors. Through Hole sensors are designed for traditional PCB mounting, offering robust performance in various applications. In contrast, SMD sensors are compact and optimized for high-density installations, enhancing thermal detection in smaller devices. Both types are utilized in diverse fields, including industrial, automotive, and consumer electronics, for temperature measurement and non-contact detection purposes, driving market growth.</p></p>
