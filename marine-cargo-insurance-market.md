<p><p>Marine cargo insurance encompasses various market types tailored to different needs. "With Particular Average" extends coverage to include partial losses, ideal for higher-value cargo. "All Risk" provides comprehensive protection against most perils, ensuring maximum security for shippers. Additionally, "Others" may include specialized policies tailored for unique goods or specific transit conditions, offering flexibility to meet diverse shipping requirements.</p></p>
